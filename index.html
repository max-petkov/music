Sunny Fruit

Sunny Fruit is a composing and production project that effortlessly moves between genres and styles, making the music sound experimental and interesting. His works range from peaceful romantic instrumentals all the way to modern EDM music, with some soul, funk and chill-hop in between. The main idea of the project is to make music that's enjoyable even after 1000 years.

Jurgen Brischar

Jurgen Brischar is a producer and multi-instrumentalist from southern Germany. His music merges Rock with Electronic and Orchestral music, resulting in energetic sounds and positive vibes. His work has been featured in several commercials and TV shows around the world.

Out of Flux

Playing with the differences and similarities between future beats and lofi, Out of Flux combines the impact of modern chord progressions with organic percussion to create a contemporary, immersive and cinematic atmosphere.
